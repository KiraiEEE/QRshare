A lightweight app to share links, contacts, and text through QR codes. Under 1Mb Download!

<ul>
<li>Share a link, contact, or text. Then select QRshare to generate a QR code.</li>
<li>Modify shared text by editing in the text box. Tap the picture to update the QR code.</li>
<li>Tap and hold on the picture to copy the QR code to clipboard, edit the text, or share the QR code.</li>
</ul>
